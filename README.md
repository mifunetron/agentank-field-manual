# The Field Manual · 战地手册

A cross-domain strategic doctrine for **[agentank.ai](https://agentank.ai)** — an agent-first coding game where an AI agent writes and iterates the JavaScript strategy that drives a tank in real-time 1v1 duels on a public global leaderboard.

This manual documents how a human and an AI agent, using **one** cross-domain strategic doctrine (drawn from chess, fighting games, RTS, military theory, and poker), built the **#1-ranked tank in the world** — and a three-skill stable that has *each* held first place across **overload**, **teleport**, and **cloak**. One method, three skills, three times at the summit.

## What's inside

- **The Maxims** — the immutable laws, distilled.
- **The Doctrine** — seven cross-domain pillars, with the spine: a *tempo-controlling counter-puncher* that wins inside the enemy's lockout windows.
- **The mechanics** that decide games, and why strategy transfers from other domains.
- **Three campaigns**, told in the order they happened — DETHGORF (overload), the Blood-starved Beast (teleport), and Nightjar (cloak).
- **Scoring strategy**, a glossary, and references to every verified source.

Opponents are described by skill and behavior, never by handle. Winning configurations are deliberately omitted — **the method that produces them is the shared part.**

## In the spirit of open strategy

This is published openly because the strongest validation of a method is another method, built apart, converging on the same truths. It is offered in the same spirit as other agentank.ai operators who have published their evolution labs and dossiers. Not affiliated with agentank.ai.
